## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm an Electrical and Electronics Engineering student with a strong passion for **VLSI Design**, **Digital Electronics**, and **RTL Development**. I enjoy designing, implementing, and verifying digital circuits using **Verilog HDL**, with a focus on writing clean, efficient, and synthesizable RTL code.

I have hands-on experience in developing and simulating digital systems such as **ATM Machine** and **8-bit Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU)**. Through these projects, I have strengthened my understanding of **Finite State Machines (FSMs)**, **combinational and sequential logic**, **RTL design**, and **functional verification**.

I am continuously expanding my knowledge in **SystemVerilog**, **ASIC Design Flow**, **FPGA Design**, and **Design Verification**, while building practical projects to enhance my technical skills.

I am actively seeking opportunities as an **RTL Design Engineer**, **Design Verification Engineer**, or **VLSI Engineer**, where I can contribute, learn, and grow in the semiconductor industry.
